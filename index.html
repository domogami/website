IEEE at UCSD

Helping Students Become Professional Engineers

About

IEEE at UC San Diego is a non-profit pre-professional student organization. We are dedicated to provide engineering students with hands-on experiences, technical and professional development and other resources to help them achieve their true potential as they develop into professional engineers.

ECE Day

ECE Day is an opportunity to celebrate, create, network, eat, and learn. During the day, you can network with alumni, create your own musical keyboard, witness fun hands-on ECE project demos, explore the new ECE classes and depths, eat free food from food trucks, and connect with other fellow students!

Learn more

Hard Hacks

H.A.R.D. Hack is a 24-hour hardware hackathon hosted by IEEE and HKN at UC San Diego. We provide the materials and the tools needed to make a hardware hack. Come out and build something cool! Anyone can attend, regardless of skill level, major, or university.
